Ice Cream Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 24 vanilla ice cream sandwiches, unwrapped
  • (8 ounce) containers whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed1
  • (12 ounce) jar hot fudge ice cream topping warmed
  • (12 ounce) jar caramel ice cream topping
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans, or to taste

Directions

ange a layer of ice cream sandwiches in the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish; top with a layer of whipped topping, hot fudge topping, and caramel topping. Repeat layering with remaining ice cream sandwiches, whipped topping, hot fudge topping, and caramel topping, ending with a top layer of whipped topping. Sprinkle with pecans. Cover dish with aluminum foil and freeze until set, at least 30 minutes.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 576 calories; 24.8 g fat; 85.4 g carbohydrates; 6.5 g protein; 40 mg cholesterol; 260 mg sodium
